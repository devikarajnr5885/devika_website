Cookie Policy

How we use cookies and tracking technologies

Last updated: January 2025

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better browsing experience by remembering your preferences and analyzing how you use our site.

Cookies contain information such as:

  • Your language preferences
  • Login status and session information
  • Items in your shopping cart or form data
  • Website usage patterns and analytics data

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly and cannot be disabled.

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
session_id Maintains user session Session
csrf_token Security protection Session
cookie_consent Remembers cookie preferences 1 year

Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website.

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
_ga Google Analytics - distinguishes users 2 years
_ga_* Google Analytics - session data 2 years
_gid Google Analytics - distinguishes users 24 hours

Functional Cookies

These cookies enable enhanced functionality and personalization.

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
theme_preference Remembers dark/light mode 1 year
language_pref Stores language selection 1 year
form_data Saves form progress 7 days

Marketing Cookies

These cookies are used to deliver relevant advertisements (only with your consent).

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
_fbp Facebook Pixel tracking 3 months
ads_preferences Ad personalization 1 year

4. Cookie Consent and Management

Your Consent

When you first visit our website, you'll see a cookie banner asking for your consent. You can:

  • Accept all cookies
  • Reject non-essential cookies
  • Customize your cookie preferences
  • Change your preferences at any time

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through various methods:

Browser Settings

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies
  • Firefox: Options > Privacy & Security > Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Cookies
  • Edge: Settings > Cookies and Site Permissions

Cookie Preference Center

Use our cookie preference center to:

  • View all cookies used on our site
  • Enable or disable specific cookie categories
  • Update your preferences at any time
  • Learn more about each cookie's purpose

5. Impact of Disabling Cookies

Disabling certain cookies may affect your website experience:

Essential Cookies

  • Website may not function properly
  • Security features may be compromised
  • Form submissions may fail
  • Session data may be lost

Analytics Cookies

  • We cannot improve website performance
  • Usage statistics will be incomplete
  • No impact on website functionality

Functional Cookies

  • Preferences will not be remembered
  • Personalization features disabled
  • May need to re-enter information

Marketing Cookies

  • Advertisements may be less relevant
  • Marketing effectiveness reduced
  • No impact on core website features
